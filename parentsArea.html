Information for Parents/Guardians

Important information and form downloads for parents/guardians.






  1. Subs
  2. Permission
  3. Health Forms
  4. Gift Aid



Subs

As you know, subs are important so that we have the funding we need in order to keep the unit unit open. The current rate is £90 for the whole year (September-June) and it is helpful if we can gain payment for the full amount by Christmas. Payment plans can be made, so please come and talk to us if you have any problems. For those with more than one daughter in the unit, we can accept half payments for each daughter, each term. For your information, there is a table below showing the breakdown of the fees.
On occassion we may ask for extra money to be paid for larger trips outside of the Guide Hall.

Reason Amount
(per girl)
Hall fees£10
Insurance£30
Council fees£25
Unit Funds£25
£90

Unfortunately we are unable to receive payment by bank transfer so please make all payments either in cash or by a cheque made payable to 1st Milngavie Guides.




Permission Slips

This section will be kept up-to-date with any permission slips which need to be filled out for upcoming outings. Please check regularly to ensure that you don't miss out! We will also be handing forms out on guide nights in case you have no access to a printer. You can either hand-in the form via your daughter to a leader, or email them straight to us.

E-mail addresses can be found on the Contact Us page.

Blank consent form which can be filled out for any event.




Health Forms

As you know, it is of utmost importance that we have up-to-date information about your daughter in the unlikely event that something should happen. This includes allergies, medical conditions, emergency contact details etc. Please make us aware of any changes to any of this information. We have a duty to keep in-line with data protection act and will not share any of this information outside of guiding. For your convenience, there is a blank health form available to download.

You can either hand-in the form via your daughter to a leader, or e-mail them straight to us.

Downloadable Health Form

healthForm.pdf
Blank health form to be used whenever updating health or emergency contact information.


Gift Aid

Gift Aid is a scheme created by the government which allows donations to be made to charity. With this scheme, any money which is given to charities-including subscriptions to guiding is eligible for tax relief provided that the donor is a tax-paying UK citizen. Gift aid is therefore a great way for our unit, to increase our funds without anyone having to pay an extra penny! All you have to do is fill out a declaration found below.
You can either hand-in the form via your daughter to a leader, or email them straight to us.
