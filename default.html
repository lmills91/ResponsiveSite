Welcome to the 1st Milngavie Guides brand new website!


Here we will be posting pictures and videos from our weekly activities, as well as outings for all parents, guardians and Guides to see what we've been upto. We will use it to communicate schedules, forms and deadlines so nobody is missed out.
There is a page especially for the parents here giving all the information you will need about fees, uniform, activities and more.
New guides should visit here to learn what it means to be a Guide and what they need to know to make their promise.
