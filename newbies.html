New Guides

This page will tell you all you need to know about starting at Guides.





The Patrols.

Guides are split into groups called patrols. Currently we have 4 patrols, but this will change based on the numbers of Guides we have. The patrols are:

The Starfish

The Penguins

The Pandas

The Parrots


Soon after starting, you will be placed into your patrol and this is the group that you will spend your time with carrying out activities in your designated "patrol time". You should use this time to work on go-for-its or badges as a group. Links to these can be found on the home page.
For more detailed information about your patrols and what you need to bring see the patrols page.

The Leaders.

There are 4 leaders and 2 young leaders who run 1st Milngavie Guides. If you have any issues at all, please speak to one of us and we will do everything we can to help you.

The Promise.

Making your promise is an important part of being a Guide. It is important that you know and understand what you are saying when you make your promise. You can be enrolled as a Guide whenever you are ready, and wherever you want, to make it a special occasion. You may do it by yourself or as part of a group. It is your decision. The leaders will help you in understanding the promise and the Guide Laws before you are fully enrolled into Guides.

I promise that I will do my best:
To be true to myself and develop my beliefs,
To serve the Queen and my community,
To help other people
and
To keep the Guide Law.

The Guide Laws.

In addition to the promise, there are also 6 Guide Laws which you should know and understand for enrolment.
These are:

  1. A Guide is honest, reliable and can be trusted.
  2. A Guide is helpful and uses her time and abilities wisely.
  3. A Guide faces challenge and learns from her experiences.
  4. A Guide is a good friend and a sister to all Guides.
  5. A Guide is polite and considerate.
  6. A Guide respects all living things and takes care of the world around her.

The Uniform.

We recommend that you don't rush out to buy the uniform until you are sure that you enjoy Guides and are going to stay. There are 2 uniforms out, the old style which can be worn until September 2016, and a new one. It is entirely your choice what uniform you wish to wear. All we ask is that you wear dark coloured bottoms with either blue, black or white shoes/trainers/sandshoes to the weekly meetings (something comfortable for running around in). For formal occasions you should wear smart black trousers and shoes, but we will inform you when these events are as they happen.


All uniform can be found on the girlguiding website here.

The Important Bit.

Most importantly you're here to HAVE FUN! We aim to plan activities that you and the other Guides will find fun and challenging. If you have any ideas of what you would like to do at Guides, let us know! It's all about you Guides getting to experience new things. If you'd rather give your ideas anonymously, you can by going to our message board. We'll check here regularly to see what you're thoughts are.
