Our Patrols

Information about each patrol including weekly schedules.







Guiding should be led by the girls, and a part of this is undertaking go for its and badges within their patrols.
Each week we will ask you to email us what you will be doing the next week so we can update this page.
If you need the kitchen, there is a first-come first-serve basis, so try to be organised.

Email your schedule and completed badge material to: 1stmilngavieguides@gmail.com
